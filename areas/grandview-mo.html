How far is Spark Doc Electric from Grandview, MO?
Grandview is one of the closest Missouri cities to our Olathe base — typically about 20–25 minutes via I-435. That means same-day service is almost always possible. For emergencies, we're available 24/7 at (913) 390-3780.
Are you licensed to do electrical work in Missouri?
Yes. We are licensed and insured to perform electrical work in both Kansas and Missouri, including Grandview. All work complies with the Missouri Electrical Code, and we handle permits and inspections through the City of Grandview whenever required.
My Grandview home has an older panel — when does it need to be replaced?
Not every older panel needs immediate replacement, but it does need to be evaluated. Signs that warrant prompt attention include breakers that trip frequently, a panel that feels warm to the touch, buzzing or burning smells near the panel, or service that's 100 amps or less with high electrical demand. We'll inspect it and tell you honestly what we find.

Yes — we serve all of Grandview including neighborhoods along Grandview Road, Blue Ridge Boulevard, and throughout the city. We also serve the areas just across the state line in Johnson County, KS, making us a great choice for the entire southern KC border region.
