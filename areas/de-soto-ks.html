How quickly can you get to De Soto, KS?
De Soto is about 15 minutes from our Olathe base — one of the closest cities we serve. Same-day appointments are almost always available. For emergencies, call (913) 390-3780 and we'll give you an honest ETA, typically well under an hour.
I'm building a new home in De Soto — can you wire it?
We handle residential electrical work including new construction wiring, panel installations, and rough-in circuits. If you're working with a builder in De Soto and need a licensed electrician for new construction, give us a call to discuss your project and timeline.
Do you pull permits for electrical work in De Soto, KS?
Yes. We handle all permit applications through the City of De Soto for work that requires one — panel upgrades, new circuits, EV charger installations. We coordinate the inspection and make sure everything passes to code. Permitted work protects your home's value and your safety.
My older De Soto home has a 100-amp panel. Is that enough?
It depends on your load. A 100-amp panel can be adequate for a smaller home without heavy draw appliances, but if you're adding an EV charger, electric dryer, HVAC upgrade, or hot tub, you'll likely want to evaluate an upgrade. We'll assess your current panel and load and give you a straight answer about whether an upgrade is necessary for what you want to do.
