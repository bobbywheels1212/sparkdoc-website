Electrician in Lee's Summit — Common Questions

Do you serve all of Lee's Summit, MO?
Yes — we serve all of Lee's Summit including downtown, Summit Woods, Chapel Ridge, the 470 corridor, and the newer developments on the eastern side of town. Lee's Summit is about 25–30 minutes from our Olathe home base, making same-day service routinely available.
My Lee's Summit home was built in the 1970s or 1980s — do I need a panel upgrade?
Possibly. Homes from that era in Lee's Summit commonly have 100-amp panels that were sized for the appliance loads of decades past. If you're adding an EV charger, a hot tub, or a major kitchen appliance, we'll evaluate whether your current panel can handle it safely or whether an upgrade to 200A makes more sense. We'll give you an honest assessment — not a sales pitch.
Can you install an EV charger at my Lee's Summit home?
Absolutely. Level 2 EV charger installation is one of our most common jobs in Lee's Summit. We handle the permit, run the dedicated 240V circuit, install the charger, and schedule the inspection with the city. Most installations are completed in a single visit.
Do you pull permits for work in Lee's Summit?
Yes, always. For any work that requires a permit in Lee's Summit — panel upgrades, new circuits, EV charger installs — we handle the permit application and coordinate the city inspection. Permitted work protects you when it comes time to sell your home and ensures the work is done to code.
