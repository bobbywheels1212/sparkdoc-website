Federal Pacific & Zinsco Panels

If you have a Federal Pacific Stab-Lok or Zinsco panel, we strongly recommend replacement. These panels have well-documented failure rates — their breakers frequently don't trip under overload, creating a serious fire hazard. Many insurers now require replacement before issuing a policy. We can diagnose your panel and give you an honest assessment.

Common Questions

Panel Upgrade FAQ

How do I know if I need a panel upgrade?
Signs include: frequently tripping breakers, a 100-amp panel with modern appliances, a Federal Pacific or Zinsco panel, no room for new circuits, adding an EV charger or hot tub, or your insurance company flagging the panel.
How much does a panel upgrade cost in Kansas City?
Costs vary based on panel size, whether the service entrance needs upgrading, and permit fees. Spark Doc Electric provides upfront pricing before starting. Call (913) 390-3780 for an accurate quote.
Do I need a permit to upgrade my electrical panel?
Yes. Panel upgrades require an electrical permit in Kansas and Missouri and must be inspected. Spark Doc Electric handles all permitting as part of the job — we pull the permit, complete the work, and schedule the inspection.
What is a Federal Pacific or Zinsco panel and why is it dangerous?
These panels from the 1950s–1980s have defective breakers that frequently fail to trip under overload, creating a fire hazard. Many insurers won't cover homes with these panels. Replacement is strongly recommended.
How long does a panel upgrade take?
Most residential panel upgrades take 4–8 hours. Your power will be off during installation. We aim to have you fully powered up by the end of the day.
