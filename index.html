What We Do

Electrical Services for Every Need

From quick repairs to full-home rewires, Spark Doc Electric handles it all — on time and to code.

Panel Upgrades & Breaker Box

Outdated or overloaded panel? We handle safe, code-compliant upgrades that protect your home and support modern electrical loads.

EV Charger Installation

Charge your electric vehicle at home with a Level 2 charger. We handle the wiring, dedicated circuit, and installation — fast.

Electrical Wiring & Rewiring

From new construction wiring to complete rewires of older homes, we bring your system up to modern safety standards.

Lighting Installation

Recessed lighting, ceiling fans, outdoor fixtures, and smart lighting solutions installed cleanly and professionally.

GFCI Outlets & Outlet Repair

Protect your family with GFCI outlets in kitchens, bathrooms, and outdoor areas. We also fix dead or faulty outlets quickly.

Dedicated Circuits

High-demand appliances need their own circuit. We install dedicated circuits for home offices, hot tubs, HVAC, and more.

Whole-Home Surge Protection

One power surge can destroy thousands in electronics. A whole-home surge protector safeguards everything in your home.

Safety Inspections

Buying a home or worried about aging wiring? Our thorough electrical safety inspections give you peace of mind and a clear action plan.

Troubleshooting & Repairs

Flickering lights, tripped breakers, mysterious outages — we diagnose and fix electrical problems fast, same day when possible.
